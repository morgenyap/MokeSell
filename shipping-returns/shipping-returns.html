1. Shipping Policy

MokeSell provides shipping services for orders placed through our marketplace. We partner with reliable couriers to ensure timely and secure delivery.

2. Delivery Times

Estimated delivery times based on your location:

  • Local Orders: 3-5 business days
  • International Orders: 7-14 business days

Please note that delivery times may vary due to unforeseen circumstances such as weather conditions or carrier delays.

3. Shipping Costs

Shipping fees are calculated at checkout based on item weight, destination, and shipping method. Free shipping may be available for selected promotions.

4. International Shipping

MokeSell offers international shipping to select countries. Customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or import fees imposed by their country.

5. Return Policy

You can return most items within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and in the same condition as received.

Exceptions:

  • Perishable items (e.g., food products) are non-returnable.
  • Personal hygiene products cannot be returned due to health regulations.

6. Refund Policy

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after receiving and inspecting the returned item. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

7. Contact Us

For any shipping or return inquiries, contact our support team at support@mokesell.com.
