Who We Are

Welcome to MokeSell - a revolutionary online marketplace dedicated to reducing food waste and promoting environmental sustainability. Our platform connects buyers and sellers, allowing businesses and individuals to trade surplus food, discounted groceries, and second-hand items at competitive prices.

Founded with a vision to make sustainable shopping the norm, MokeSell provides a reliable and user-friendly platform for people to shop smarter, save money, and make an impact on the environment.

Our Mission: Reducing Food Waste, One Sale at a Time

Every year, millions of tons of food go to waste due to overproduction, mislabeling, or minor imperfections. At MokeSell, we believe that perfectly good food should not be wasted. Our mission is to bridge the gap between food suppliers, supermarkets, and consumers by offering , surplus, and near-expiry grocery products that would otherwise be thrown away.

By reducing food waste, we are helping to lower carbon emissions, decrease landfill waste, and support a circular economy. We work closely with suppliers, wholesalers, and food producers to ensure that high-quality food products are given a second chance before they reach the landfill.

Why Choose MokeSell?

  • Eco-Friendly Shopping: Buy near-expiry and surplus food at low prices while reducing waste.
  • Budget-Friendly Deals: Get discounted groceries, household items, and pre-owned goods.
  • Community-Driven: Join a marketplace that supports sustainable living and responsible consumption.
  • Wide Variety: From fresh produce to electronics, find unique items at competitive prices.
  • Secure Transactions: Shop with confidence on our trusted platform.

How We Support Sustainability

At MokeSell, we go beyond just selling products - we advocate for a greener future. Our partnerships with sustainable brands, eco-conscious businesses, and food rescue organizations allow us to take real action against waste and pollution. We also offer incentives for sellers who list sustainable and environmentally friendly products.

A Subsidiary of Sheng Siong Group

MokeSell is proudly a subsidiary of Sheng Siong Group, one of Singapore's leading supermarket chains. With Sheng Siong's extensive retail experience, we ensure that our platform upholds the highest standards of trust, security, and customer satisfaction.

Join Us in Making a Difference

Whether you're a consumer looking for affordable groceries or a business seeking to reduce waste, MokeSell provides the perfect solution. By choosing us, you're not just shopping - you're making a positive impact on the planet.
